Introduction

From a very young age, I have been taught the fundamentals of art from my professional artist aunt, and I grew up fascinated with a graffiti-style design. My first professional setting that allowed me to use this artistic hobby was working for IPMD as the product engineer. Combined with my experience in circuitry and hardware, I used Autodesk Maya to generate production quality schematics of our personalized health device.
My second experience was while working for UCSF Department of Neurology. With my passion for graphic design and data visualization, I have published multiple figures in couple of amazing scientic journals. This is one that I have worked on with my mentor Andres while I was working at UCSF. Check out the link to the paper!

The second animation is one that I created for another coworker, Alex, explaining the importance of myelination in fixational eye motion abnormalities.
